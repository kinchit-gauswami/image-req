## ðŸ“¸ Photography Tips

### If Taking New Photos at RiseUp Events:
- **Get consent** from participants for photography
- **Wide shots work best** - Shows scale and engagement
- **Capture authentic moments** - Real learning and collaboration
- **Multiple angles** - Have options for different compositions
- **Natural lighting** when possible
- **Professional attire visible** - Shows serious career focus
- **Technology props** - Laptops, presentations add context
- **Group dynamics** - Collaboration and mentorship visible

### Privacy Considerations:
- Wide/medium shots where individuals aren't too identifiable
- Side angles or back angles maintain privacy
- Focus on activities rather than faces
- Group shots work better than close-ups
- Blurred backgrounds can help
