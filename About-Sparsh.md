## ðŸ’¡ Photography Tips

### For Atmiya Sparsh Events:
- **Capture genuine moments** - authentic smiles and engagement
- **Show diversity** - different age groups and activities
- **Wide shots work well** - group activities and community feel
- **Natural lighting preferred** - or well-lit indoor venues
- **Action shots** - people actively participating
- **Maintain dignity** - respectful distance and angles

### Privacy Considerations:
- Ensure you have permission to use photos of people
- Consider using shots from behind or side angles
- Group shots where individuals aren't too identifiable work well
- Focus on activities rather than close-ups of faces if privacy is a concern
