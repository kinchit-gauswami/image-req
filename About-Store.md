## ðŸŽ¨ Image Guidelines

### Requirements:
- **Quality:** High resolution, professional photography
- **Lighting:** Well-lit, vibrant colors work well
- **Composition:** Can be busy/detailed since it's used as background
- **Colors:** Warm, inviting tones preferred
- **Subject:** Store-related, spiritual items, or devotional theme

### Good Options:
1. **Product Display** - Books, murtis, and portraits arranged attractively
2. **Devotional Items** - Close-up of spiritual items with bokeh background
3. **Store Setting** - Clean, well-lit store area or preparation space
4. **Abstract/Pattern** - Spiritual symbols, mandala, or decorative patterns
5. **Haridham Interior** - Beautiful architectural or decorative elements

### What Works Best:
- Images with **good contrast** (will have dark overlay)
- **Warm colors** (oranges, golds, purples work well)
- **Interesting textures** or patterns
- **Professional quality** photography
