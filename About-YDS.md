## üé® Image Guidelines

### General Requirements:
- **Quality:** High resolution, professional photography
- **Lighting:** Well-lit, avoid harsh shadows
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- **Colors:** Vibrant but natural colors
- **File Size:** Optimize for web (compress without losing quality)

### Photography Tips:
1. **Avoid blurry or pixelated images**
2. **Use proper white balance** (avoid overly orange or blue tints)
3. **Keep horizons straight**
4. **Consider rule of thirds** for composition
5. **Remove any distracting elements** if possible

### For Guruhari Photos:
- **Respectful framing** - Ensure dignified presentation
- **Clear face visibility** - Well-lit, in-focus
- **Traditional attire** - As appropriate
- **Background** - Clean or spiritual setting
- **Quality** - Professional photography preferred

### Optimization Tools:
- **TinyPNG** (https://tinypng.com/) - Great for reducing file size
- **Squoosh** (https://squoosh.app/) - Google's image optimizer
- **ImageOptim** (Mac) - Lossless image compression
